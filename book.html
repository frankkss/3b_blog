Book Ni Sydrick

This book highlights the stories, achievements, and experiences that define the CCS journey by Sydrick Parra.

Within the halls of CCS, students embark on a journey filled with challenges, discoveries, and achievements. Some master programming, others excel in research, and then there’s Sydrick—grinding ranks in Valorant while the professor is mid-lecture. Through dedication, collaboration, and the occasional “ACE!!!” echoing across the room, CCS students cultivate skills that prepare them for the ever-evolving world of technology.

This book is more than just pages filled with stories—it’s a chaotic yet inspiring tribute to the passion, perseverance, and brilliance of CCS students. Whether you’re a current student, an alumnus, or someone who admires both technology and tactical shooters, Book Ni Sydrick will entertain, motivate, and make you question your classroom’s noise levels. Don’t miss out—get your copy today!
