Miss ko na Siya

Falling for a classmate is pretty much inevitable when you see each other every day. You share notes, group projects, and suddenly, you're head over heels. But just like that, things can go downhill—whether it's because of a quiz, a missed text, or a group assignment gone wrong. The good news? There’s always a chance for a plot twist—just make sure it doesn’t happen during finals week!

And while it's easy to fall in and out of "classroom love," the real challenge comes when you realize you're both fighting for the top spot in the group project. One minute, you're handing over the last piece of the puzzle, and the next, you're dodging awkward glances during break time. Ah, the sweet romance of student life!

But hey, even if it’s a little rocky, don’t worry. You can always bounce back, especially when the group chat turns into a place for memes and inside jokes again. Who knows? The next quiz might just bring you closer... or maybe that plot twist will strike again!
